I am a last-year Ph.D. student in the Italian National Artificial Intelligence Program co-organized by the University of Pisa, Italy, and the University of Trento, Italy. I am in the Multimedia and Human Understanding Group (MHUG) advised by Prof. Dr. Nicu Sebe and Prof. Dr. Rita Cucchiara. During my Ph.D. time, I also spent time at ETH Zürich with Prof. Dr. Luc Van Gool and Dr. Yawei Li, and at INSAIT Sofia with Prof. Luc Van Gool and Dr. Danda Pani Paudel. Prior to my Ph.D. studies, I received my master's degree under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Hong Liu in computer science, Peking University, China, and received my B.Eng. degree (2016) in the College of Mechanical and Electrical Engineering, Central South University, China.

My research interests lie in the intersection of computer vision, and deep learning. Motivated by exploring more informative deep representations by Vision Transformers (i.e, ViTs, and I really like Transformer!), I have worked mainly on tasks like: Low-Level Vision (2D), Scene Understanding (3D), and Multimodal Reasoning for various interesting applications.
