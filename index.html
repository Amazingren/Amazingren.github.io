I am a Postdoctoral Associate at MBZUAI, mentored by Prof. Dr. Salman Khan and working closely with Prof. Dr. Fahad Shahbaz Khan on World Model🌍. I achieved my Ph.D. degree in Artificial Intelligence from the Italian National Artificial Intelligence Program co-organized by the University of Trento, Italy and the University of Pisa, Italy under the joint supervision of Prof. Dr. Nicu Sebe and Prof. Dr. Rita Cucchiara. During my Ph.D. time, I spent time at ETH Zürich as visiting Ph.D. Scholar with Prof. Dr. Luc Van Gool. I also work closely with Prof. Dr. Ming-Hsuan Yang. Prior to my Ph.D. studies, I received my master's degree under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Hong Liu from Peking University, China, and received my B.Eng. degree from Central South University, China.

My research interests lie in the intersection of computer vision, and deep learning. Motivated by exploring more informative deep representations by Vision Transformers (I like ViTs!), I have worked mainly on tasks like: Low-Level Vision (2D), Scene Understanding (3D), and Multimodal Reasoning for various interesting applications.
