Bin Ren (任斌)

I am a third(last)-year Ph.D. student in the Italian National Artificial Intelligence Program co-organized by the University of Pisa, Italy, and the University of Trento, Italy. I am in the Multimedia and Human Understanding Group (MHUG) advised by Prof. Dr. Nicu Sebe and Prof. Dr. Rita Cucchiara. Previously, I was an academic visiting student at the Computer Vision Lab (CVL) of ETH Zürich, working closely with Dr. Yawei Li under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Luc Van Gool. Currently, I am a visiting Ph.D. student at INSAIT, Sofia, working closely with Dr. Danda Pani Paudel and Prof. Dr. Luc Van Gool on LLMs.

My research interests lie in the intersection of computer vision, and deep learning. Recently I have worked on Low-Level Vision, and 3D Gaussian Splatting. In addition, I've also begun exploring multi-modal LLMs like Llama and LLaVA.

Prior to my Ph.D. studies, I received my master's degree (2021) under the supervision of Prof. Hong Liu in computer applied technology, Peking University, China, and received my B.Eng. degree (2016) in the College of Mechanical and Electrical Engineering, Central South University, China.
